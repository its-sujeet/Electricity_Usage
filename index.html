Tips for Saving Energy at Home

  • Turn off lights when you leave a room.

  • Unplug electronics when you're not using them, even if they're turned off.

  • Weatherize your home to make it more energy-efficient. This includes sealing air leaks and adding insulation. You can also install energy-efficient windows and doors.

  • Switch to energy-efficient appliances and light bulbs. Energy-efficient appliances use less energy and can save you money on your energy bills.

Tips for Saving Energy at Work

  • Turn off lights when you leave a room, even at work.

  • Unplug electronics when you're not using them, even at work.

  • Set the thermostat to a lower temperature in the winter and a higher temperature in the summer.

  • Turn off the water when you're not using it, even at work.



  • Print on double-sided paper and recycle used paper at work.
